FAQs

What is Nearby?

Nearby is a mobile app that allows you to borrow/buy/lend/sell items with people around you.

How do I start using Nearby?

Download Nearby from the app store or play store, login using either Google or Facebook, fill out your profile, and add payment info. After that, you can start creating requests & making offers!

Does Nearby cost money to use?

Nearby is free, however we keep 14% + $0.30 of profits. If you loan out a tent for $10, we will keep $1.70 and you will make $8.30.

How do I get paid?

Payments are handled through the app using Stripe. Your first transaction may take about a week to appear in your bank account, but after that you should typically see credits within a couple days of a completed transaction.

What if someone doesn’t return my item?

Please reach out to us ASAP! You can email us at info.nearby.app@gmail.com and we will attempt to reach out the the buyer. If the buyer doesn’t respond, we will charge them the estimated cost of the product, replace your product, and ban them from the app. We will do everything we can to to make the situation right, but sellers do assume some risk by loaning out their items.

What if someone breaks my item?

We hope that the two people involved in the transaction can work something out, but if not, please reach out to us and we will do everything we can to make it right.

What kind of items can be shared on Nearby?

Just about anything, though it must be an item. We would recommend items under $300 - kitchen appliances, tools, camping gear, sporting goods, electronics, clothing...the list goes on. Nearby is not for loaning out your child, dog, or skills. You may not share drugs, guns, or other illegal substances. We encourage our users to flag anything they find inappropriate.

Can I report someone?

Yes, you can "flag" content in the app and "block" a user. We will review all flagged content in a timely manner and ban users from the app who we feel have acted innappropriately. You may also contact us at info.nearby.app@gmail.com at any time with questions or concerns.

What if I forgot to verify the exchange by scanning the QR code?

If you are the person who transferred the item (the seller on the initial exchange & the buyer on the return), navigate to your history page, select the appropriate transaction card, and click “exchange”. In the lower right hand corner you should see some text that says “Forgot?”. Select that text then enter the approximate time the item was exchanged. The other person involved in the transaction will be prompted to verify that the exchange occurred.

Can I loan out my stuff for free?

Absolutely! Just enter in $0 when you make the offer.

Is nearby safe to use?

While we do require our users to login through an existing account (Google or Facebook) and require them to give us personal information such as their phone number & payment info, you should always use caution when meeting strangers. We encourage people to meet in public places, bring along a friend, and only accept offers from people who have profile pictures.

Will I have to pay taxes on items that I sell?

If you make over $20,000 in charges prior to fees AND have more than 200 transactions in a given year, you will have to pay taxes. We will provide you with a 1099 if this is the case.
